# Understanding tradeoffs

Every architectural decision involves tradeoffs. The key is making them explicit and choosing deliberately.

## Common tradeoffs

- **Consistency vs. availability**: Can you wait for consistency, or do you need immediate availability?
- **Simplicity vs. flexibility**: Do you optimize for now or for future changes?
- **Performance vs. maintainability**: How much complexity are you willing to accept for speed?
- **Centralization vs. distribution**: Where should logic live?

## The tradeoff test

If you can't explain what you're giving up for what you're gaining, you haven't thought through the tradeoff.
